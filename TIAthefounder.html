About Thaha Islamic Academy

Shaykh Thaha Al-Hassan Al-Edoowy

Initiated through the endeavours of Shaykh ‘Thaha Al-hassan and actualized through the efforts of his closest team members, Thaha Islamic Academy is a multilevel organization that seeks to spread Islamic Knowledge throughout the world. Thaha Islamic Academy exists to raise the level of knowledge amongst the Muslims in Edo-State (Nigeria) and abroad. Our Institution also seeks to provide much needed and affordable counseling to Muslims and help the youth be confident in their Islamic Identity in these troubling times. With the guidance of The Qur’an and Sunnah, we hope to provide light in these dark times and elevate the level of knowledge of the Ummah, with the permission of Allah ‘Aza wa Jal

The Aim of T.I.A :

Thaha Islamic Academy is an eminent website where you are given the opportunity to learn within the comfort of your Home or wherever you may be without any form of stress or discomfort. This mahoosive opportunity is brought to you by a young Nigerian orator from Edo state by the name Thaha Al-hassan, He established this beneficial site; to make it easier for every individual who have the keen to learn more about islaam and all that guides it. The founder made this Academy an online-kind so as to make it easier for anyone in Nigeria and abroad to be able to study this islamic knowledge without having to risk their lives or live their loved ones is this creepy era we found ourselves we ask Allah ‘Azza wa Jall to preserve the founder and make it easy for anyone who want to join the Academy. Ameen.
